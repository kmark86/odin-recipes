Lazy Ramen

Description

A bowl of lazy ramen. Perfect for breakups.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Chicken Bullion
  • Soysauce
  • Chicken Thigh with skin on
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Pan fry chicken thigh
  2. Boil Water
  3. Slice green onion
  4. In serving bowl, put chicken bullion, soysauce, green onions, and rendered chicken fat
  5. Add ramen to boiling water
  6. Add ramen to serving bowl, laddel in some water to make soup
