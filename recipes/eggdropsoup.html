Egg Drop Soup

Description

A bowl of egg drop soup. Perfect for breakups.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cornstarch
  • Chicken Bullion
  • Soysauce
  • Egg
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Boil water with corn, chicken bullion, and soysauce
  2. Beat egg in a bowl
  3. Mix little water with cornstarch
  4. Once boiling, add cornstarch slurry while mixing
  5. Stir in egg
  6. Serve in bowl
